## Chimera Chives Stuffed Turkey

A turkey stuffed with aromatic Chimera Chives.

Ingredients:

* 1 Turkey (3-4 kg)
* 100g Chimera Chives
* 30g Centaur Cilantro

Preparation steps:

1. Season the turkey with Centaur Cilantro.
2. Stuff the turkey with Chimera Chives.
