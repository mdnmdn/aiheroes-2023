## Harpy Honey Glazed Ham

Sweet and sticky ham with a touch of the sky.

Ingredients:

* 1 Ham (1.5 kg)
* 100g Harpy Honey
* 50g Ghostly Gouda

Preparation steps:

1. Score the ham's skin.
2. Glaze with Harpy Honey and sprinkle with Ghostly Gouda.
